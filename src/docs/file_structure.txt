mods/HytaleFoundations/
├── main.json                (Global settings: Language, DebugMode, Maintenance)
├── commands.json            (Enable/Disable specific commands, aliases)
├── permissions.json         (Default rank permissions)
└── modules/                 <-- MODULAR CONFIGS (Easy to navigate)
│   ├── economy.json         (Currency name, starting balance)
│   ├── chat.json            (Formats, banned words, channel radii)
│   ├── protection.json      (Spawn protection flags, world rules)
│   └── discord.json         (Webhooks, bot tokens)
│
├── data/                        <-- PLUGIN STORAGE (Do not edit manually)
│   ├── players/                 <-- PLAYER DATA (Split & Modular)
│   │   ├── usermap.json         (UUID <-> Name index)
│   │   └── [UUID]/              (Folder per player)
│   │       ├── profile.json     (Identity, settings)
│   │       ├── stats.json       (Kills, deaths, playtime)
│   │       ├── economy.json     (Balances)
│   │       ├── mail.json        (Messages)
│   │       └── homes.json       (Home locations)
│   │
│   └── server/                  <-- GLOBAL DATA
│       ├── warps.json           (Warp locations & metadata)
│       ├── kits.json            (Kit items & cooldowns)
│       ├── spawns.json          (Spawn points per world)
│       └── regions.json         (WorldGuard-style region data)
│
├── lang/                        <-- LOCALIZATION
│   ├── en-us.json
│   └── es-es.json
│
└── logs/                        <-- AUDIT LOGS
    ├── admin_actions/           (Command usage, bans, kicks)
    ├── economy/                 (Transactions, pay, shops)
    └── chat/                    (Chat history by date)
